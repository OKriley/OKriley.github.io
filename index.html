La La Land

Synopsis

Sebastian (Ryan Gosling) and Mia (Emma Stone) are drawn together by their common desire to do what they love. But as success mounts they are faced with decisions that begin to fray the fragile fabric of their love affair, and the dreams they worked so hard to maintain in each other threaten to rip them apart.

Director

Damien Chazelle is an American director and screenwriter. His breakthrough came when he wrote and directed his second feature film, Whiplash (2014), which was based on his award-winning 2013 short film of the same name. In 2016 his film La La Land received critical and commercial acclaim, winning all 7 of its Golden Globe nominations, including Best Director and Best Original Screenplay. It also received a record-tying 14 Academy Award nominations, winning six including Best Director for Chazelle who became the youngest person in history to win a Oscar for Best Director at the age of 32.

Genre

La La Land is a romantic musical drama that blends elements of classic Hollywood musicals with contemporary storytelling.It pays homage to the Golden Age of musicals with vibrant cinematography, elaborate dance sequences, and a jazz-infused score, while also incorporating elements of romance and drama. The film balances the whimsical and nostalgic aspects of traditional musicals with a more bittersweet, realistic narrative, making it a modern yet timeless take on the genre.
